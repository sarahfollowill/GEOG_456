Bar polar charts are helpful to visualize the magnitude of a data trend to see proportionally how data has changed over time; however, it is not the easiest chart to read, especially when there is not much data, and it does not often make sense to display data radially.

I personally don't think that this chart is best for displaying my data as it is difficult to determine the year for each data entry. I also chose the reds colorscale to show urbanization becoming more extreme over time as the red becomes darker.
