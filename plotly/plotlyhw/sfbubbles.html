Bubble charts are very visually pleasing and are well-equipped to display a difference in data proportion, as is shown in my project, and display how data has trended over time. They are limited, however, in that the dataset cannot be too large and bubbles cannot be too big otherwise the chart might become difficult to interpret. Here, the slight bubble overlap is useful to show just how extensively the data has changed, but if there was more data it might become confusing.
